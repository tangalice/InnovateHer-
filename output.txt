Kotex

* Beneficial Ingredients: Kotex products may include beneficial ingredients such as absorbent materials like cotton and cellulose, which help to absorb menstrual flow and keep users dry.
 
* Harmful Ingredients: Some Kotex products have been found to contain harmful ingredients like dioxins, fragrances, and dyes, which can cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, and other health problems.

* Potential Side Effects: Potential side effects of using Kotex products include vaginal irritation, itching, and burning, as well as more serious health issues like toxic shock syndrome, although this is rare.

* Safer Alternatives: Safer alternatives to Kotex include menstrual cups, reusable cloth pads, and organic cotton tampons and pads from brands like Natracare, The Honest Company, and Seventh Generation.

* Ranking: Ranking: 3, as while Kotex products are generally considered safe to use, they may contain some harmful ingredients and have potential side effects, but the company has made efforts to reduce the amount of these ingredients in recent years.
